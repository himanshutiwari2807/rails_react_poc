Overview

This SCORM module provides a comprehensive introduction to SCORM standards and their implementation in e-learning courses. SCORM, which stands for Sharable Content Object Reference Model, allows for the interoperability, accessibility, and reusability of web-based learning content.

Features

  • Easy to integrate with LMS
  • Supports tracking of learner progress
  • Interactive and multimedia content
  • Complies with industry standards

Benefits

  • Enhances learning experience
  • Ensures content compatibility across platforms
  • Facilitates detailed analytics and reporting
  • Improves content reusability and maintenance
